Room

The Perfect Room for Your Perfect Getaway

Matoshree Resort Room

The accommodation consists of six tents, including a large tent designated for conferences and meetings, making it an ideal location for business gatherings or events. One of the tents is equipped with 20 beds, while there is also a honeymoon tent available upon request, ensuring a personalized and romantic experience for couples. Guests can enjoy free Wi-Fi throughout the area, keeping them connected at all times. Each room comes with extra mattresses, wake-up call services, an LCD TV, and coffee and tea facilities. Additionally, the site offers a bar and restaurant, where guests can unwind and enjoy delicious meals, along with room service for added convenience and comfort.

The tents are fully equipped with modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay. These include a 21-inch LCD screen TV, with an additional 52-inch LCD screen TV located in a separate tent for those looking for a more expansive viewing experience. Cable channels are available for entertainment, and each tent has its own individual air conditioning unit, allowing guests to adjust the temperature to their preference. For added convenience, the accommodation offers both smoking and non-smoking rooms, catering to all guests' needs. Whether for a business trip, honeymoon, or leisure stay, these well-appointed tents provide a perfect blend of comfort and functionality.
